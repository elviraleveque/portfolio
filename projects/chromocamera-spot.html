About

ChromoCamera spot is a promotional spot for the app verison of ChromoCamera (see the website project here).


The main character, Jessie, is on the sofa in her house and she's looking at her old pictures of her, but she is visibly embittered by her outfits. Suddenly the smartphone comes to life, and it transports Jessie into the ChromoCamera app, where she will be guided through its use.
